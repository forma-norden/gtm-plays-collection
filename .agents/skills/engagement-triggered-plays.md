# engagement-triggered-plays

Use this skill to design "warm" outbound plays that trigger when a prospect
interacts with marketing content, webinars, or social posts.

## 1. The "Webinar Q&A" Play
**Target:** Attendees who asked a question or answered a poll during a live event.
**Timing:** Less than 2 hours post-event. (Speed is everything).
**Channel:** Email -> LI Connect.
**Avoid:** "Thanks for attending our webinar! Here is the recording. Want a demo?"
**Angle:** "Saw you asked about [Topic] during the Q&A today. Have a deeper framework on exactly how to solve that specifically for [Their Industry]. Worth sending over?"

## 2. The "Content Download" Play (eBook/Report)
**Target:** Anyone filling out a Lead Gen form or website form for an asset.
**Timing:** Less than 15 minutes.
**Channel:** Automated Email from Rep -> Manual LI follow-up day 2.
**Avoid:** "Hope you enjoyed the eBook! Can we jump on a call?"
**Angle:** "Just sent the report over. The graph on page 14 regarding [Metric] caught my eye—are you guys also seeing that trend at [Company]? Curious how you're handling it."

## 3. The "LinkedIn Commenter" Play
**Target:** ICP contacts who leave a meaningful comment on a company or founder post.
**Timing:** Same day.
**Channel:** LinkedIn DM.
**Avoid:** Pitching immediately.
**Angle:** "Appreciate the comment on my post about [Topic]. Your point regarding [Their Comment] was spot on. Are you guys currently running into that bottleneck over at [Company]?"

## 4. The "High-Intent Page Viewer" Play
**Target:** Returning visitors who spent >60 seconds on the /pricing or /demo page (identified via RB2B or Clearbit).
**Timing:** Within 4 hours.
**Channel:** Email to the likely buying committee (CEO, VP Sales, etc., depending on ICP).
**Avoid:** "I saw you on our pricing page." (Creepy).
**Angle:** Treat it like cold outbound, but prioritize this account above all others today. "Usually, companies your size start looking at [Category] when [Trigger Event] happens. If you're currently evaluating options, we do X..."

## Execution Constraints
- Inbound engagement != Inbound intent. They want the content, not your software.
- The goal of these plays is to start a conversation about the *topic of the content*, not to immediately pitch a demo.
- Lead routing speed is critical. If a rep doesn't follow up on a webinar lead within 24 hours, the lead is functionally dead.

## Output Contract
When designing an engagement play, provide:
- The required automation routing (e.g., n8n -> HubSpot -> Outreach).
- The SLA (Service Level Agreement) for SDR follow-up speed.
- A 2-step soft-touch sequence focused entirely on the content topic, not the product.
