- **Play Type:** [Signal / Cold Sequence / Engagement / Expansion]
- **Target Audience:** [Specific Persona + Company Size + Vertical]
- **Owner:** [Who built it / maintains it]
- **Status:** [Draft / Active / Paused / Retired]

## 1. The Trigger
*What initiates this play?*
- **Data Source:** [e.g., Apollo Funding Alerts]
- **Specific Filter:** [e.g., Series B or C, raised in last 14 days]
- **Validation:** [How does the SDR double-check the trigger is real?]

## 2. The Premise
*Why are we reaching out right now?*
- **The Core Pain:** [e.g., Post-funding, boards expect immediate scalable revenue. Manual CRMs break.]
- **Our Value Prop:** [e.g., We automate CRM hygiene so reps spend 20% more time selling.]
- **The "So What":** [e.g., If you don't fix this, your CAC will spike and the board will panic.]

- **Channel:** [Email/LinkedIn/Call]

- **Channel:** [Email]
